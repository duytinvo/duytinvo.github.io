Dr. Tin D. Vo is currently an assistant professor at the department of Computer Science of Lakehead University (Ontario, Canada). After obtaining the PhD degree in Computer Science under the supervision of Professor Yue Zhang at Singapore University of Technology and Design (Singapore) in 2017, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Laval University (Quebec, Canada) in one year and a data scientist at Chata Technologies (Alberta, Canada) in two years. Before that, he also worked as a junior lecturer at Cantho University in two years after getting his Bachelor degree there in 2011. His research interests include natural language processing (NLP), machine learning (ML), deep learning (DL) and artificial intelligence (AI). He is recognized in the field by having several publications on the top conferences in NLP (i.e. ACL, EMNLP, CICLing) and AI (i.e. AAAI, IJCAI). He has been currently focusing on applying ML and DL into text classification, topic modelling, sentiment analysis, toxicity detection, semantic parsing, machine translation and text generation.
